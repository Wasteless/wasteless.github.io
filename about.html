About us

Nils Christian Haugen

Nils Christian enjoys helping teams deliver software that makes both the users and the sponsors happy. He has been practicing agile software development since 2000 as a developer, architect, team lead and coach as a consultant for ThoughtWorks and as CTO for Know IT Objectnet.

Nils Christian is frequently invited to talk at companies, user groups and universities, and has presented at international conferences such as Agile, JavaZone, XP Day London and SPA. He has helped out organizing several of the JavaZone conferences and is now one of the enthusiast behind the Smidig conference.

Stein Grimstad

Stein has more than 10 years' industry experience as software developer and solution architect. His professional interests cover many areas of software development, but is particularly related to the interaction between clients and vendors. He received a phd on software cost estimation from the University of Oslo, Norway in 2006, and is a member of the estimation project at Simula Research Laboratory.

Stein has been the author of several papers published in international journals and magazines. Topics include expert estimation, process improvement and evaluation of software technology. He frequently talks at international conferences, give courses, and work with industrial partners. He is one of the key organizers of JavaZone.
